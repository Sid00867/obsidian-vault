Here's a concise reading list, ordered roughly from **most relevant** to **background**.

### Foundation / Generalist Policies

1. **Foundation Policies with Hilbert Representations (HILP)** (ICML 2024) — Park, Kreiman, Levine. ([Proceedings of Machine Learning Research](https://proceedings.mlr.press/v235/park24g.html?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Foundation Policies with Hilbert Representations"))
    
2. **Coverage as a Principle for Discovering Transferable Behavior in Reinforcement Learning** (ICLR 2021) — Campos et al. ([OpenReview](https://openreview.net/forum?id=INhwJdJtxn6&utm_source=chatgpt.com "Coverage as a Principle for Discovering Transferable Behavior in Reinforcement Learning | OpenReview"))
    

### Transferable Exploration

3. **Leveraging Skills from Unlabeled Prior Data for Efficient Online Exploration (SUPE)** (ICML 2025) — Wilcoxson et al. ([Proceedings of Machine Learning Research](https://proceedings.mlr.press/v267/wilcoxson25a.html?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Leveraging Skills from Unlabeled Prior Data for Efficient Online Exploration"))
    
4. **Gap-Dependent Unsupervised Exploration for Reinforcement Learning** (AISTATS 2022) — Wu et al.
    

### Unsupervised RL / Skill Discovery

5. **Diversity is All You Need (DIAYN)** (2018) — Eysenbach et al. ([arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/1802.06070?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Diversity is All You Need: Learning Skills without a Reward Function"))
    
6. **Dynamics-Aware Unsupervised Discovery of Skills (DADS)** (2020) — Sharma et al.
    
7. **Constrained Ensemble Exploration for Unsupervised Skill Discovery** (ICML 2024) — Bai et al.
    
8. **Unsupervised Reinforcement Learning for Transferable Manipulation Skill Discovery** (2022) — Cho et al. ([arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2204.13906?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Unsupervised Reinforcement Learning for Transferable Manipulation Skill Discovery"))
    

### Exploration & Open-Endedness

9. **Go-Explore: A New Approach for Hard-Exploration Problems** (2019) — Ecoffet et al.
    
10. **Curiosity-driven Exploration by Self-supervised Prediction** (ICM) (2017) — Pathak et al.
    
11. **Large-Scale Study of Curiosity-Driven Learning** (2018) — Burda et al.
    
12. **Variational Information Maximizing Exploration (VIME)** (2016) — Houthooft et al.
    
13. **Empowerment-driven Exploration** — Mohamed & Rezende (2015)
    

### Foundations / Theory

14. **The Option-Critic Architecture** (2017) — Bacon, Harb, Precup.
    
15. **Discovery of Intrinsic Options** (DIRO) (2021).
    
16. **Unsupervised Reinforcement Learning: A Survey** (survey paper).

### If your specific interest is **"foundation exploration policies"**, I would start with just these five:

1. **Foundation Policies with Hilbert Representations (HILP)** ([Proceedings of Machine Learning Research](https://proceedings.mlr.press/v235/park24g.html?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Foundation Policies with Hilbert Representations"))
    
2. **Coverage as a Principle for Discovering Transferable Behavior**
    
3. **SUPE: Leveraging Skills from Unlabeled Prior Data for Efficient Online Exploration** ([Proceedings of Machine Learning Research](https://proceedings.mlr.press/v267/wilcoxson25a.html?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Leveraging Skills from Unlabeled Prior Data for Efficient Online Exploration"))
    
4. **DIAYN**
    
5. **DADS**
    

Those five are the closest existing work to the idea of learning a **general, transferable exploration prior** rather than a task-specific policy.
